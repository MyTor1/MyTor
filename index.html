1. Introduction

Welcome to MyTor ("we", "our", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app. By using MyTor, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the minimum information needed to provide our services.

For regular users:

  • Phone number (for login and OTP verification)
  • Name
  • Booked appointments (date and time)
  • Authentication tokens (JWT and refresh tokens) used to keep your account logged in securely

For business owners:

  • Business name and details (open/close hours, services, prices)
  • Business contact number and location
  • Logo and image (optional)
  • Appointments booked by users

We do not collect payment details, emails, or unnecessary personal data.

3. How We Use the Information

We use your information only to:

  • Manage appointments between users and businesses
  • Display business details and availability
  • Send appointment reminders via push notifications
  • Improve and secure our app experience
  • Perform OTP verification for login and authentication

We do not share, sell, or rent any user data to third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is securely stored in our database with strict access control.

Authentication tokens (JWT and refresh tokens) are securely stored on your device using encrypted storage mechanisms. They are used to maintain your session without exposing your personal data.

Notifications are stored locally on your device and never sent to our servers.

5. Account Deletion and Logout

You can delete your account and all related data at any time in two ways:

  • Through the "Delete Account" button in the app's settings
  • By contacting us at MyTor00@hotmail.com

Once deleted, all your data (appointments, personal info, business info) will be permanently removed.

Logging out of the app will:

  • Delete all local authentication tokens (JWT and refresh tokens)
  • Invalidate any server-side refresh tokens

This ensures your session is completely terminated.

6. Children's Privacy

MyTor is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any third party, except when required by law (e.g., legal request).

8. Your Rights

You can request:

  • To view or correct your data
  • To delete your account and data permanently
  • To stop receiving notifications (via device settings)

9. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be published in the app, and continued use means you accept the updated terms.
